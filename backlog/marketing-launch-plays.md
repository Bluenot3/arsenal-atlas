## Launch Order

1. Launch the AI Business Space Launch Kit as the main conversion offer.
2. Use Smart Solution Plan as the lead magnet and diagnostic hook.
3. Add Proof Vault as the trust layer in every sales call.
4. Hold Night Shift as the premium automation upsell until credit lifecycle is verified.
5. Recruit partners after the proof bundle exists, so they have something concrete to sell.
6. Use AI Cost and Routing Audit for technical buyers, agencies, and teams with existing AI spend.
