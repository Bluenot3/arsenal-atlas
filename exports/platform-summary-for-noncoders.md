# Platform Summary For Noncoders

Arsenal is a business operating system for AI work. It has digital workers (Agents), business workspaces (Business Spaces), a creation engine (Z-Engine), a prompt/instruction lab (Instruction Lab), a memory/proof vault (Files/Vault), an execution engine (Flows/Night Shift/Flex OS), a marketplace/distribution layer, and a business control plane (Admin).

The biggest money path is Business Spaces. A business starts with a pain point, gets a Smart Solution Plan, sees ROI, receives a proposal, activates agents and automations, gets outputs, and pays through subscriptions, setup fees, or credits.

The biggest trust path is AI Route. AI Route helps Arsenal control providers, credits, limits, fallbacks, and logs. Some features still need migration or clearer exception labels.

The biggest product challenge is clarity. Arsenal has a lot of powerful pieces. Atlas turns those pieces into a map so Alex, Codex, designers, marketers, and investors can understand what exists and what to improve next.
