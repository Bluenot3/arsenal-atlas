# Non-Coder Platform Guide

Arsenal is easiest to understand as an operating system for business work.

## The Operating System

An operating system connects apps, files, users, permissions, and background processes. Arsenal does the same for AI work. It connects agents, files, automations, business plans, proposals, public pages, billing, credits, and admin controls.

## The Command Center

The dashboard helps builders decide what to create next. The admin dashboard helps ZEN control users, billing, credits, AI providers, feature flags, usage, audit logs, pages, partners, and Business Spaces.

## The Digital Workforce

Agents are digital workers. They need a job description, instructions, tools, knowledge, memory, a model, and a deployment channel. Arsenal lets those workers live inside the app, on public pages, embeds, and external channels.

## The Business Factory

Business Spaces are the factory floor. A business pain point becomes a Smart Solution Plan. The plan becomes ROI. ROI becomes a proposal. The proposal becomes agents and automations. Work creates outputs. Outputs become proof, reports, portals, and exports.

## The Vault

Files/Vault is the memory of the platform. It stores uploaded knowledge, generated work, replays, reports, proposals, prompt packs, and client deliverables. For a business buyer, the Vault is proof that AI work happened and can be reused.

## The Marketplace

Marketplace, Discover, Arsenals, Prompt Packs, Skills, and Experiences are the future distribution engine. Creators can build assets, users can discover or buy them, creators can earn, and Arsenal can take a commission.

## The Control Plane

AI Route is the control plane for AI. It decides which model/provider is used, how credits are charged, how failures fall back, and what admins can see.
