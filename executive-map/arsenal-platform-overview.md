# Arsenal Platform Overview

Arsenal is ZEN AI Co.'s Smart Business Solutions OS: a unified platform for turning business needs into deployable agents, workflows, files, client deliverables, and measurable revenue systems. The inspected app is not just a chatbot builder. It includes agents, Business Spaces, Z-Engine, Instruction Lab, Vault, Skills, Flows, Arsenals, Marketplace/Discover, public pages, partner revenue, programs, credits, subscriptions, and a broad admin control plane.

## Platform Primitives

- Agents: deployable digital workers with instructions, models, knowledge, actions, channels, public pages, and sharing.
- Business Spaces: B2B operating spaces that combine profile, ROI, plans, agents, automations, Night Shift, Flex OS, proposal, portal, and outputs.
- Z-Engine: advanced creation and reasoning studio for reusable systems and artifacts.
- Instruction Lab: prompt/instruction refinement, Tune-powered tasks, prompt packs, and reusable instruction assets.
- Files / Vault / Knowledge: memory and proof layer for files, generated outputs, reports, replays, proposals, and knowledge.
- Skills: reusable capabilities that can attach to agents and future marketplace assets.
- Automation / Flows / Schedules / Baskets: repeatable execution and operational containers.
- Arsenals: bundles of agents, pages, products, and experiences that can be published and monetized.
- Marketplace / Discover / Community: distribution and network-effect surfaces.
- Admin Dashboard: control plane for users, billing, credits, providers, AI routes, flags, usage, audit, partners, pages, and operators.

## What Is Confusing

- Many routes, aliases, and legacy/labs surfaces make the platform hard to explain without maps.
- Some AI features use `ai-route`, while others use direct provider calls or dedicated routes.
- Marketplace monetization pieces exist, but paid listing and creator payout loops need clearer packaging.
- Knowledge retrieval, web search, and some dedicated generators need clearer admin visibility for provider, credit, and usage behavior.

## What Should Be Fixed First

- Migrate or explicitly classify remaining direct AI/provider paths.
- Add debit/refund/idempotency tests around credits and high-cost media/business automation paths.
- Create a clean buyer journey for Business Spaces -> proposal -> paid plan -> execution.
- Convert Marketplace into one end-to-end paid asset path before broad creator-economy marketing.
- Keep Labs/legacy routes out of normal user journeys.
