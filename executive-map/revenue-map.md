# Revenue Map

## Revenue Paths

- Subscriptions: Free, Plus, Pro, Premium via PricingPage, create-checkout, stripe-webhook, check-subscription, and tier limits.
- Credits: credit_accounts, credit_ledger, credit_settings, credit_topup_packs, create-credit-topup-checkout, and route-level credit debits.
- Credit top-ups: reserve credit packs backed by top-up checkout/webhook paths.
- Business Spaces: preview on Free; execution/export/proactive work on Pro/Premium; natural path to setup fees, monthly retainers, and credit usage.
- Smart Solution Plans: AI-generated business plans that convert pain into ROI and proposal-ready work.
- Paid Arsenals: public Arsenal pages with paid access checkout and access grants.
- Experiences: generated interactive tools with entitlement and usage paths.
- Skills: reusable capabilities that can become paid marketplace assets after review/risk workflow is stronger.
- Prompt packs: prompt pack generation, install, likes, and future creator sales.
- Marketplace commission: creator publishes, user discovers, user pays, creator earns, Arsenal takes commission.
- Affiliate/referral revenue: partner dashboard, referral settings, partner commissions, Stripe Connect support.
- Done-for-you services: Business Spaces, Client Studio, Admin Pages, proposal/export, and Z-Engine can be packaged as agency services.
- Agency/white-label packages: Premium tier includes white-label; admin pages and public Arsenals support branded delivery.
- Enterprise plans: admin audit, usage, provider controls, feature flags, and operator observability are enterprise proof points.

## Highest-Leverage Revenue Order

1. Package Business Spaces as the flagship Pro/Premium B2B product with setup-fee and monthly service tiers.
2. Sell Smart Solution Plans as a lead-to-proposal engine for agencies and SMB operators.
3. Turn Night Shift into a premium automation/credits SKU after debit/refund tests are locked.
4. Use AI Route provider routing to protect margin and make credits predictable.
5. Launch paid Arsenals as productized bundles with paid access grants and creator payouts.
6. Make Files/Vault proof exports a sales deliverable for clients and agencies.
7. Create a first paid marketplace flow for prompt packs or agent templates before broad marketplace claims.
8. Bundle admin pages/client studio into done-for-you launch page and client portal services.
9. Use referral rewards and Stripe Connect to grow distribution without paid ads.
10. Position Experiences as paid interactive tools once entitlement/usage paths are tightened.
