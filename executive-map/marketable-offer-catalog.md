## Packaging Notes

- Use setup fees where the offer includes implementation, configuration, client onboarding, or proof capture.
- Use monthly tiers where the offer includes ongoing usage, credits, operators, channels, storage, governance, or support.
- Use revenue share where creators, partners, or marketplace assets become the distribution channel.
- Keep public claims focused on visible proof assets until remaining risks are verified.
