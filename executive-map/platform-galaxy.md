# Platform Galaxy

Arsenal has 28 mapped feature clusters. The gravitational center is the combination of Agents, Business Spaces, AI Route, Files/Vault, Billing/Credits, and Admin.

## Core Orbit

- Agents create digital workers.
- Business Spaces package those workers around real business outcomes.
- Files/Vault stores proof and reusable assets.
- AI Route governs model usage, cost, fallback, and usage logs.
- Billing/Credits convert usage into revenue.
- Admin keeps the system controllable.

## Growth Orbit

- Marketplace distributes assets.
- Arsenals package assets into products.
- Experiences turn AI outputs into usable tools.
- Prompt Packs and Skills become creator inventory.
- Referrals and partners expand distribution.

## Trust Orbit

Audit logs, RLS, usage events, provider controls, credit ledger, customer portal, and screenshot-safe demos create enterprise trust.
